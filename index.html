What's this site?

Maybe you're wondering what's the purpose of this site?

It's a fantasy lore website, where I explain some fantasy world I've been working last months.

It's meant to be a free fantasy worldbuilding for other artists, writers, game developers, to serve as inspiration so they can make their own derivative ideas.

My goal with this lore is to evolve into something beyond my initial ideas, similar to how linus never imagined linux will become, if he gave the freedom of others to edit and use his works, free of copyright issues.

So, please enjoy my ideas I tried to turn into some cool lore to work from.
